Love & Hate Cleaners is a property cleaning service targeting homeowners, renters, and landlords who need reliable, affordable help keeping their properties clean — inside and out. The name says it all: we love getting it done, and we hate the mess just as much as you do.

💡 Taglines

  • ⭐ #1 "We Hate the Mess So You Don't Have To"
  • 🥈 #2 "Love the Results. Hate the Mess. That's Our Job."
  • 🥉 #3 "From Overgrown to Outstanding — Every Time"
  • 4 "Spotless Properties. Zero Excuses."

⚠️ Two-Division Structure

One of the smartest things about this business is recognizing that it actually operates as two separate service divisions — because the logistics, tools, and customers are different enough to treat them independently.

🌿 Division 1 — Outdoor / Yard Services

Lawn cleanup, leaf removal, hedge trimming, debris hauling, yard restoration, post-construction outdoor cleanup.

Equipment Needed

  • Lawn mower / weed trimmer
  • Leaf blower
  • Hedge clippers
  • Rakes, tarps, hauling bags
  • Truck or trailer for debris

Who the Customer Is

  • Homeowners who don't have time or physical ability to maintain their yard
  • Landlords with rental properties between tenants
  • Homeowners prepping to sell (curb appeal)
  • HOA-compliant neighborhoods with seasonal cleanup needs

🏠 Division 2 — Indoor / Home Cleaning Services

Deep cleaning, move-in/move-out cleans, regular maintenance cleaning, garage cleanouts, post-construction interior cleanup.

Equipment Needed

  • Cleaning supplies (mops, vacuums, surface cleaners)
  • Microfiber cloths & scrub brushes
  • Disinfectants
  • Trash bags & organizing bins

Who the Customer Is

  • Renters moving in or out of apartments/homes
  • Homeowners preparing for guests or events
  • Property managers turning over rental units
  • Families needing one-time deep cleans

💰 Revenue Model & Pricing

Payment structure: Half payment upfront, half upon completion. Free estimates available before any commitment.

Basic Yard Cleanup (small)$75 – $150

Full Property Cleanout$250 – $500+

📣 Marketing Strategy

  • Word of mouth — neighbors, family friends, church community
  • Social media — post before/after photos on Instagram and Facebook
  • Website — live and accessible for all customers
  • Flyers — distribute in neighborhoods and apartment complexes
  • Referral program — discount for customers who refer new clients

🏁 Vision for the Future

As the business grows, the plan is to:

  1. Separate the two divisions into officially branded sub-services
  2. Hire 1–2 helpers per division to scale operations
  3. Build a repeat client base with monthly and bi-weekly cleaning contracts
